The visualization on the right is a connection between 'subjects' and 'emotions'. We use the dataset of Reddit from 2014 to 2017 and select all the subreddicts that people care about a lot in their lives, for example: family, friends, work and etc. We also select the emotional subreddicts and get mainly two kinds of emotions: postive and negative.
